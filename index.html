Methodology & FAQ

How is break-even calculated?

For each month we compare the running total of subscription spend against the total cost of owning the box — the financed hardware payments (principal plus interest), plus electricity and any optional operating costs you enable. The break-even month is the first month where cumulative ownership cost drops below cumulative subscription cost.

What costs are excluded by default?

By default the model excludes maintenance/depreciation, hardware resale value, and sales tax. Each can be toggled on in the Optional assumptions section of the calculator. The model is cost-only — it does not rate model quality or coding performance, and it is not financial advice.
